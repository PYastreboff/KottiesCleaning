Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book your cleaning services?
Booking our services is easy! Simply visit our website and click on the "Book Now" button. Follow the prompts to select your service type, preferred date and time, and provide your contact information. It takes less than a minute to secure your appointment.
What happens if I need to reschedule or cancel my appointment?
We understand that plans can change. If you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment, please notify us at least 48 hours in advance, and we'll be happy to accommodate your request. However, cancellations made within 48 hours of the scheduled appointment may incur a fee. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we strive to be flexible and accommodating to your needs.
Are your cleaning professionals trained and insured?
Yes, all our cleaning professionals are highly trained and fully insured for your peace of mind. We prioritize the safety and security of both our clients and our team members.
What should I expect during a spring cleaning service?
Our spring cleaning service is a comprehensive, top-to-bottom deep clean of your space. We focus on areas that may not receive regular attention, such as baseboards, ceiling fans, and behind appliances. It's designed to refresh and revitalize your home, leaving it spotless and rejuvenated.
Can I choose the cleaning products you use?
Absolutely! We understand that some clients have specific preferences or sensitivities. During the booking process, you can specify any particular cleaning products or brands you'd like us to use, and we'll do our best to accommodate your requests.
